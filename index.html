The Philosophy

Omaccy is built on the belief that your computer should work for you, not the other way around. Maybe you've been tempted by Linux with its infinite customization, but you're unwilling to get rid of your MacBook. You love your AirPods. You love iMessage. You love AirDrop. You just can't stand to downgrade to a non-Apple Silicon chip. You want the customizability of Linux without throwing the baby out with the bathwater. Omaccy is here for you.

This setup is intentionally opinionated: it provides a curated set of automations and sensible configurations, so you can get productive quickly and focus on what matters. It's designed to keep your hands on the keyboard. It’s designed to be easy to install, easy to update, and easy to reset. It's also designed to be easy to understand and easy to modify.

The Features

Omaccy is primarily built on top of Hammerspoon and PaperWM. Hammerspoon is a powerful automation engine for macOS that lets you script and control nearly every aspect of your system, from window management to hotkeys and beyond. PaperWM is a tiling window manager extension for Hammerspoon, inspired by the PaperWM project for GNOME, which brings a horizontal, scrolling window manager to your Mac.
